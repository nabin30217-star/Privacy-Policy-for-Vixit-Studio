# Privacy Policy for Vixit Studio **Last Updated:** January 21, 2026 ## 1. Introduction Welcome to **Vixit Studio** (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application (the **“App”**) handles information when you use our services. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. --- ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Personal Information We **do not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable information (PII), such as: - Name - Email address - Phone number --- ### 2.2 Media and Files Vixit Studio is a video editing and conversion tool that processes media files stored on your device. - **Local Processing Only:** All video, audio, and image editing operations (including conversion, compression, merging, and reversing) are performed **entirely on your device** using local libraries such as **FFmpeg**. - **No Uploads:** Your media files are **never uploaded** to our servers or to any third-party cloud services. --- ### 2.3 Usage Data and Diagnostics We may use third-party services (such as advertising or analytics providers) that collect **non-personally identifiable information** to help improve app performance and display ads. This data may include: - Device type and model - Operating system version - Advertising ID (AAID) - Network connection type - General app usage data (e.g., session statistics, crash reports) --- ## 3. Permissions We Request The App requests only the permissions necessary to function properly: - **Media Access (Photos / Videos / Audio or Read & Write Storage):** Required to access your media files and save edited results to your device. - **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`):** Used to notify you when long-running tasks (such as video export) are completed. - **Foreground Service (`FOREGROUND_SERVICE`, `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROCESSING`):** Allows the App to continue processing videos in the background without interruption. - **Wake Lock (`WAKE_LOCK`):** Prevents the device from sleeping during intensive video processing. - **Internet (`INTERNET`):** Used **only** for displaying advertisements and checking for app updates. **Media files are never uploaded.** --- ## 4. Third-Party Services ### 4.1 Google AdMob We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data such as your device’s advertising ID to show relevant ads. - Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy You can manage ad personalization through your device settings or via the consent form shown in the App (where legally required). --- ### 4.2 Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) We use **Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)** to collect user consent for personalized advertising in regions where required by law (such as the EEA and the UK), in compliance with **GDPR**. --- ## 5. Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect your data: - **Offline Processing:** Your media files stay on your device at all times. - **Secure Libraries:** We rely on trusted, industry-standard libraries (such as FFmpeg) for safe and efficient processing. --- ## 6. Children’s Privacy The App is **not intended for children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
