Privacy Policy for Vixit Studio

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Vixit Studio (“the App”) is developed by Vixit Studio (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Media and Local Processing

Vixit Studio is a video editing and conversion tool. All video, audio, and image processing (including conversion, compression, merging, and reversing) is performed locally on your device using libraries such as FFmpeg.

Your media files are never uploaded to our servers or to any third-party cloud services.

3. Usage Data and Advertising

The App may use third-party services (such as Google AdMob) to display advertisements and improve app performance. These services may collect non-personally identifiable information, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Network connection type
  • General app usage data and crash reports

4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Media Access (Photos / Videos / Audio or Storage): To read media files and save edited results to your device.
  • Notifications: To notify you when long-running tasks such as video exports are completed.
  • Foreground Service & Wake Lock: To allow uninterrupted video processing in the background.
  • Internet: Used only for displaying ads and checking for updates. Media files are never uploaded.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising and Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect user consent where required by law (such as GDPR in the EEA and UK).

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since all media processing is performed offline on your device, your files remain under your control at all times.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page or within the App.
